iKON, One of K-Pop's Top Artists

The Beginning of iKON

Anyone who knows the K-Pop industry knows it to be cutthroat and unforgiving, and iKON, a once 7 member group is no stranger to it.
Debuting on September 15, 2014 under YG Entertainment, the idol boy group consists of Jay(Kim Jinhwan), Song(Song YoonHyung),
Bobby(Kim JiWon), DK(Kim DongHyuk), Ju-ne(Koo JunHoe), Chan(Jung ChanWoo), and Former member B.I.(Kim Hanbin).

Before debuting and becoming iKON, Yang Hyunsuk, YG's CEO at the time, put them through two survival shows to fight for their debut. They were
introduced as Team B to the public on the first show in 2013, "WIN: Who is next", and although they had lots of popularity already from public trainee
dance and vocal practices, this show gained them even more fans. Fighting through lots of tears and desperation to debut, they narrowly lost to
Team A, who is currently known as "Winner". Yang Hyunsuk told them that this loss would cost them their debut together and they had little hope
of staying together. They continued training despite this detour in hopes that they could debut even if they were separated, and Yang HyunSuk
answered their prayers by suddenly deciding to hold another survival show called "Mix and Match" in 2014, and he surprised them by adding
other trainees to up the competition. Again, through sleepless and stressfull days of showing off their rap, dance, and vocal skills, the 7 members who
are in the picture above were chosen to debut. Fans eargerly waited for their debut in 2015 to show off their hard work.
